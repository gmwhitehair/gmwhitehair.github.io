Short Answer 1

Prompt

Explain what 'Math.floor(Math.random()*3+1)' does in the JavaScript when we added it to our Rock,Paper,Scissors game. What did it allow us to do?

Response

In my rock paper scissors game we assigned 1 to paper, 2 to rock, and 3 to scissors. The (Math.random()*3+1) gives us a number from 1 to 4, inclusive of 1, exclusive of 4. Then that Math.floor function rounds that number down to 1, 2, or 3 so we can assign it to rock, paper, or scissors.
